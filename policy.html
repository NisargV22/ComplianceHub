Password Policy

Purpose: Establish minimum requirements for password creation, storage, and management. Scope: All employees, contractors, and systems handling company data.

Requirements

  • Minimum 12 characters, no forced periodic rotation without cause (NIST 800-63B aligned)
  • MFA required for all privileged and remote access accounts
  • Passwords must not be reused across systems

Control Mapping

This policy satisfies the following controls:

  • AC-2 (CIS Controls v8): Account Management

  • IA-5 (CIS Controls v8): Authenticator Management

Enforcement & Exceptions

Violations are addressed per the Acceptable Use Policy. Exceptions require written approval from the Security team and must be reviewed quarterly.
